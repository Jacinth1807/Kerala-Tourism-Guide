Places of Interest

Step into Kerala's enchanting tapestry of lakes, rivers, and bustling streets and embrace Kerala's charm where every waterway and street corner tells a tale of timeless allure.

Vagamon

A serene hill station known for its rolling green meadows, lush pine forests, and cool, misty climate.

The Chinese Fishing Nets

A unique sight in Fort Kochi, these giant, cantilevered fishing nets are a legacy of ancient trade relations with China.

Athirapally Waterfalls

Often called the 'Niagara of India,' these magnificent waterfalls are located on the Chalakudy River.
