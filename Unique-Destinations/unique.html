Unique Destinations

Explore the hidden gems of Kerala with a journey through its unique destinations, offering a blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, adventure, and immersive experiences.

Poovar

A stunning coastal village where a lake, river, and sea meet, creating a beautiful golden sand beach and floating cottages.

Muzhappilangad Drive-in Beach

India's only drive-in beach, offering a unique opportunity to drive along the shoreline of the Arabian Sea.

Varkala

A lesser-known coastal town with dramatic red cliffs and pristine beaches, perfect for a peaceful getaway.
