Art & Culture

A land where ancient traditions blend seamlessly with contemporary creativity. Discover Kerala's artistic heritage that continues to inspire and enchant visitors from around the globe.

Kathakali

Witness this spectacular classical dance form known for its elaborate costumes, vibrant makeup, and expressive gestures.

Kalaripayattu

The world's oldest martial art, Kalaripayattu is a thrilling display of ancient combat techniques and physical prowess.

Theyyam

A mesmerizing ritualistic art form from North Kerala, where performers transform into deities.
